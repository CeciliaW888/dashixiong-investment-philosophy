📚 核心概念

🏗️ 底层逻辑：一切的基石

想象你要买一栋房子。你会只看外墙的颜色就下单吗？当然不会。你会查地基、查结构、查社区规划。投资也是一样。底层逻辑就是投资的「地基」。

Imagine buying a house. Would you buy based only on the paint color? Of course not. You'd check the foundation, structure, and neighborhood plans. Investing works the same way. 底层逻辑 is the "foundation" of any investment.

大师兄举了中国主题ETF的例子：软件、人工智能、电信、信息安全、云计算、芯片——这些听起来都是「风口」「赛道」。但看月K线图，2023年中冲上顶点后，全部垮下来了。为什么？

⚠️
大师兄的警告：「在这样一个宏观经济的阶段，去掉毒瘤，然后开始慢慢的从受损当中恢复，是没有底层逻辑去大量的崩崩崩往上的。他还躺在病床上呢，你让他出去跳了？你让他玩极限运动了？」

The metaphor is vivid: the economy is a patient recovering from surgery (removing the "tumor" of 30 years of real estate monetization). A patient in a hospital bed can't do extreme sports. Similarly, an economy recovering from balance sheet damage can't support massive upward spikes in theme stocks.

🎯 90%的人在猜 90% of People Are Guessing

大师兄讲了一个训练操盘手的经典故事：

老师！我在最低点150买入了！最高点到了10000！
你是第一个要被批评的人。
为什么？我买在最低点啊！
因为你90%是猜的，90%是跟风的，90%是听消息的。看似150了，看似最低点了——接下来可能就是30。

This is the core paradox: buying at the bottom feels right, but without understanding why it's the bottom, you're just gambling. And gambling, as 大师兄 says, means bankruptcy is just a matter of time.

📖 关键术语

底层逻辑
dǐ céng luó jì
Fundamental logic — the root mechanism behind why something works or fails.
韭菜
jiǔ cài
"Leeks" — retail investors repeatedly harvested by market cycles they don't understand.
长期持有
cháng qī chí yǒu
Long-term holding — the strategy advocated by ETF creators, Buffett, and 大师兄 alike.
三分法
sān fēn fǎ
The Three-Part Method — 大师兄's risk allocation framework. Never risk what you can't lose.
宏观经济
hóng guān jīng jì
Macroeconomics — the big-picture forces (monetary policy, national debt, trade) that drive everything.
